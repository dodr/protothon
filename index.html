Say Hello to Protothon!

Protothon is a hackathon for creatives and coders seeking to invent the future of digital communication through creative collaboration.

#protothon1

21 January 2012

Theme
Spotify API

Venue
Berghs School of Communication

Spotify has opened up their API's. This is exciting. But what can we actually do with them?

Protothon will explore the creative potential of this in a 12 hour event where inter-disciplinary teams of creatives and coders will prototype their asses off to invent the answer.

Some fine people from Spotify will be present at the event to learn us all we need to know about Spotify apps.
